ABOUT IT LIVE

The first annual Iraqi technology gathering established in 2017. It is concerned with keeping up with and transferring the latest technologies around the world in the field of programming, networks, artificial intelligence, data security and digital transformation by hosting various relevant experiences and presenting ideas and strategic solutions that stimulate the technical community and decision makers to advance the reality of technology in Iraq. Local and international companies and influential Iraqi, Arab and international personalities participate in the conference annually in the field of technology and human development, within a renewed youth atmosphere that witnesses a wide turnout from the technical masses represented by state ministries, representatives of private sector institutions, university cadres, students and all those interested in this field. The conference includes encouraging and honoring paragraphs for emerging projects and innovations The conference stopped during the Corona pandemic after three purposeful and successful seasons And it resumes its activity again in the fourth season, during the month of May 2022
